About us

Framcie's Flowerpots is a business located in Western Australia. All of our pots are handmade using quality materials and techniques. We deliver to anywhere in the world. A semple of our range of pots can be viewed from the Gallery page. Other types of pots or custom pots are available catalog or more information about what's in the Gallery.

Francie began making her own pots in the early 90s and began selling them to interested friends as a hobby. Since then, the business has grown and we have a team of skilled potters working to please our customers. We're certain that we have just the right pot for you.
